This is the course's real Python λ-engine — α/β/η-reduction and Church encodings — compiled to WebAssembly with Pyodide and driven by xterm.js. Nothing runs on a server; after the first load it works offline. Write terms with λx. x or \x. x; bare integers become Church numerals, and names like AND, SUCC, PLUS, Y expand to their λ-definitions. Use ↑/↓ for history.
