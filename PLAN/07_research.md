# Module 07 — Research dossier

**Goal:** the depth, citations and current-landscape groundwork behind every lecture, so the notes are
authoritative and up to date. Produced by the `atp-course-research` workflow (6 lecture dossiers + a
2024–2026 AI-for-proof landscape sweep + a foundations/canon + four-prover comparison), adversarially
fact-checked, then synthesized into a master plan.

## Outputs (written to `research/`)
- `research/RESEARCH_REPORT.md` — synthesis: through-line, prerequisite graph, tiered reading list,
  per-lecture abstracts + session plans, cross-language artifact plan, verified tool versions.
- `research/dossiers/l1..l6.md` — per-lecture deep dossiers (objectives, topics, defs/theorems, refs,
  pitfalls).
- `research/dossiers/landscape.md`, `research/dossiers/foundations.md`.
- `research/fact_checks.md` — the verdicts on volatile claims (versions, dates, benchmarks).

## Subtasks
- [x] Launch the research workflow (background).
- [ ] Materialize workflow output into `research/*` files.
- [ ] Propagate corrections into `MEMORY.md` (versions), the book, and the landing page.

## Acceptance criteria
- Every "current fact" on the landing page / L6 traces to a fact-check verdict with a URL.
