---
title: Beta-reduction
tags: [l2, core]
---

The one computational step: `(λx.t) u →β t[x := u]`. A term with no redex is a **normal form**. Normal-order (leftmost-outermost) reduction finds a normal form if one exists.

See also: [[substitution]], [[church-rosser]], [[eta-reduction]], [[normal-form]].
