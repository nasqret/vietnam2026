---
title: Lambda term
tags: [l2, core]
---

A **lambda term** is `x` (variable), `λx.t` (abstraction) or `t u` (application). The whole of computation is built from this grammar.

See also: [[alpha-equivalence]], [[beta-reduction]], [[free-and-bound-variables]], [[church-encoding]].
