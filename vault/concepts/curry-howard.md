---
title: Curry–Howard correspondence
tags: [l1, l3, core]
---

Propositions **are** types; proofs **are** terms. `→` function, `∧` product, `∨` sum, `⊥` empty, `∀` Π, `∃` Σ; β-reduction is proof normalization.
